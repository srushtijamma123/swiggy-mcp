# **Refund Policy**

Thank you for using **Swiggy**. We aim to provide a smooth and satisfying food delivery experience. This Refund Policy explains when refunds may be issued and how users can request them.

---

## **1. Eligibility for Refunds**

Refunds may be issued under the following circumstances:

### **a. Order Not Delivered**

You are eligible for a refund if:

* Your order was not delivered to the provided address.
* The delivery partner marked the order as delivered, but you did not receive it (subject to investigation).

### **b. Incorrect or Missing Items**

Refunds or partial refunds may be provided if:

* The restaurant delivered incorrect items.
* One or more items are missing from the order.
* Food delivered is completely different from what was ordered.

### **c. Quality Issues**

Refunds may be considered when:

* The food is spoiled, stale, or unsafe for consumption.
* Packaging is damaged to a level that affects food quality.

*Note: Photographic evidence may be required.*

### **d. Payment-Related Failures**

Refunds are applicable when:

* Your payment was deducted, but the order failed.
* Duplicate payments were charged for a single order.

---

## **2. Non-Refundable Situations**

Refunds will **not** be issued in the following cases:

* Customer changed their mind after placing the order.
* Delay caused by factors outside Swiggy’s control (traffic, weather, restaurant load).
* Incorrect delivery address provided by the customer.
* Minor variations in food taste, presentation, or quantity.

---

## **3. How to Request a Refund**

To request a refund, follow these steps:

1. Open the **Swiggy App** → Go to **Help**.
2. Select the relevant **Order**.
3. Choose the issue such as:

   * “Order not delivered”
   * “Wrong or missing items”
   * “Food quality issue”
4. Submit required details or photos.
5. Wait for confirmation from the support team.

---

## **4. Refund Processing Time**

* **UPI / Wallet:** 24–48 hours
* **Credit / Debit Card:** 3–7 business days
* **Net Banking:** 2–5 business days
* **Swiggy Money credits:** Instant

*Processing time may vary depending on your bank or payment provider.*

---

## **5. Partial Refunds**

In cases where only part of the order is affected (e.g., one missing item), Swiggy may issue a **partial refund** equivalent to the value of that item.

---

## **6. Abuse of Refund Policy**

Swiggy reserves the right to:

* Suspend accounts involved in repeated or fraudulent refund claims.
* Deny refunds that violate this policy or show misuse.

---

If you have additional concerns or need help with a refund, you can reach Swiggy Support via:
